ABOUT

My name is Aura Violet, and I see fashion as a true form of art. A Paris born image stylist, I love creating visual worlds through editorials, photoshoots, and creative collaborations where clothing, colors, and textures become a language to express emotions that words alone cannot convey.

I work with both sensitivity and precision, always striving to bring unique and poetic worlds to life. Each project is, for me, an opportunity to meet other artists, share ideas, and turn a vision into a tangible creation with authenticity and passion.
